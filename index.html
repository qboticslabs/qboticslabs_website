Who we are?

We are a group of young robotics engineers who are passionate about building and programming robots. We are having expertise in various areas in robotics such as robotics simulations, algorithms, robotic vision, deep learning, speech processing and the list goes on. We can handle both research and commercial projects. We are also providing robotic education to students and professional to build next robotics engineers.

Our mission

We are dreaming the world where robots and human are co-existing and solving problems. We would like to create more job opportunities in robotics and like to provide robotics education to people to empower them to understand possibilities of robotics technology. We would like to use robotics technology for good.

Our skills

Our team is capable of doing various aspect of programming a robot. Our main capabilities include robotic simulations, robot modeling, robot perception and robot intelligence. The main development is using C/C++ and Python and we make use the robotic framework such as Robot Operating System and computer vision libraries such as Open-CV and PCL.

June 10 , 2017

This is the 3rd time we are attending ICRA and launching our book. This time we could met great professors and entrepreneurs working in Robotics. We could launch the book by creator of ROS itself. Released our 3rd book ROS Robotics Projects.

March 28 , 2017

Excited to announce our third book in Robotics for teaching how to do robotics projects using Robotics Operating System. The book is having more than 14 projects which very relevant to the current scenario.
